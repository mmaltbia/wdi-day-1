Michelle's Passion Project

For the past 3 years I have worked in the Infection Control/Patient Safety department at a hospital. A major focus within the department was in regards to how we collect and monitor hand hygiene data across the organization. The web application that my former employer currently uses has a number of drawbacks and was extremely cumbersome in regards to data entry and aggregation. I would like to build a Hand Hygiene Data Management web app that is user friendly and highly customizable.
